## Page 6: Group Members (Instructions 4)

> Each group member receives an endowment of **10** tokens.

> Each group member must decide how many tokens to allocate to the **Group Account** and how many tokens to keep in their **Private Account**.

> The number of tokens must be a whole number between **0 and 10**.

> All decisions are made in private.

---

## Page 7: Accounts (Instructions 5)

> **Group Account**
> Tokens contributed to the Group Account are added together to form a total amount. This total is multiplied by **1.8** and then evenly split among all **4** group members.

> **Private Account**
> Each member keeps the tokens remaining in their Private Account after contributing to the Group Account. These tokens are **not multiplied**.

---

## Page 8: Leader (Instructions 6)

> **Leader**

> The leader's task is to send a recommendation at the start of each round, suggesting how many tokens each group member should contribute to the group account.

> Group members are free to follow or ignore this recommendation.

> The leader will not make a contributing decision to the group account and instead will receive a payment of half of the total contributed group account.

> Leader payments do not come out of the group account.

---

## Page 9: Examples

> The following examples illustrate how payoffs are calculated.

### Leader Payoffs

> Regardless of how many tokens group members contribute to the Group Account, the Leader always receives **20 tokens**.

### Group Member Payoffs

> **Example 1:**
> You allocate 4 tokens and the other participants allocate 3, 5, and 8 tokens.
> Total contribution: 4 + 3 + 5 + 8 = 20 tokens
> 20 × 1.8 = 36 tokens, so each member receives 36 / 4 = 9 tokens
> You keep 6 tokens, so your total payoff is: 6 + 9 = **15 tokens**

> **Example 2:**
> You allocate 10 tokens and the other participants allocate 0, 0, and 0 tokens.
> Total contribution: 10 + 0 + 0 + 0 = 10 tokens
> 10 × 1.8 = 18 tokens, so each member receives 18 / 4 = 4.5 tokens
> You keep 0 tokens, so your total payoff is: 0 + 4.5 = **4.5 tokens**

> **Example 3:**
> You allocate 0 tokens and the other participants allocate 6, 6, and 6 tokens.
> Total contribution: 0 + 6 + 6 + 6 = 18 tokens
> 18 × 1.8 = 32.4 tokens, so each member receives 32.4 / 4 = 8.1 tokens
> You keep 10 tokens, so your total payoff is: 10 + 8.1 = **18.1 tokens**

> The Leader receives half of the total contributions to the Group Account. This payment does not come out of the Group Account.

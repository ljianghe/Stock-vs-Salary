The experiment is conducted by researchers associated with the Interdisciplinary Experimental Laboratory (IELab) at Indiana University and follows strict no deception policy.

In accordance with IELab rules, whatever we tell you is indeed true. For example, if we tell you that something will happen if you choose a given option, then that thing will happen if you choose that option. Anything else would violate IELab policies. For more information on this policy, see https://ie.lab.indiana.edu/conducting-experiments

Leader

The leader's task is to send a recommendation at the start of each round, suggesting how many tokens each group member should contribute to the group account.

Group members are free to follow or ignore this recommendation.

The leader will not make a contributing decision to the group account and instead will receive a payment of half of the total contributed group account.

Leader payments do not come out of the group account.

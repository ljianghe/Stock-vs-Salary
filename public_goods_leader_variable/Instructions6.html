Leader

The Leader sends a message at the start of each round, recommending how many tokens group members should contribute to the Group Account.

Group members are free to follow or ignore this recommendation.

The Leader does not make a contribution decision.

The Leader receives {{ C.LEADER_FIXED_PAYMENT }} tokens in each round, regardless of how much group members contribute to the Group Account.

This payment is not taken from the Group Account.
